Everyone will face failure in life at different stages. No matter how careful we are. Failure knocks our door at times. One who learns from their mistakes and accepting failure as a stepping stone and part of life experience will achieve great success in life. MGM Anand is one such person , who believes in the above words, Although he is a great business man who has been running business profitably for years he too faced failure.
