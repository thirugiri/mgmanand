MGM Group

                 The MGM Group is a multifaceted economic conglomerate engaged in Logistics, Hospitality International trading, Housing and Distillery and Indian-Made Foreign Liquor (IMFL) business. MGM Group is one of the leading Indian private business groups, renowned for its efficient administration, commitment to quality and comprehensive response to the respective markets. The talent shows and the artistes in the MGM are done by the local community people. The MGM group gave importance to the welfare of the people around them. MGM Anand believes that we will not be remembered for how rich we are but for our good deeds. He is aware of the truth that money is temporary. The MGM group under his leadership doing various welfare aids irrespective of communal favoritism. Some of their unbiased philanthropic works are; old age homes, education scholarships, funding the destitute who possesses a talent for business.
