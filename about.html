Early Life

                 MGM Anand esteems his School days. He did his schoolings in St. Mary’s school Royapuram. He learned many valuable morals during his school times. He became interested in Sports and developed contented blithesome friendships. He engrossed himself in sports, especially athletics like sprint, hurdles, javelin throw and own an orange belt in Karate. His passion for Karate comes from the classic movie Enter the Dragon. Through his perseverance in Sports he understood that patience and hard work are a must for success, failure is not the end, erring is humanely it can be amended.His supreme mentors are his Father and his elder brother MGM Maran, they helped him to master the technique of handling their megacorporation. Although he is born with a silver spoon, his early years in business were not facile.



Business

           He entered the family business when he was just 16 as a vehicle Ticket (VT) inspector. His position as VT helped him understood the concerns of various factions; he connects with each and everyone, regardless of their position. He believes that the confederation of the management with its workers is crucial for the business advancement. At the age of 25, he initiated his father’s business in a new approach. At first his approach seemed unorthodox, but it was a great success. He builds a crude MIS database to collect and process report; long before modern software enabled systems are created. His efficient changes brought the company significant success. The Cargo Company inaugurated by his father in the year 1963 was about to shut down with around 500 vehicles including trucks, excavators and Dozers. MGM Anand with his sensible thinking he proposes to use the vehicles in road construction and river excavation. This helped the business group to involve in the pivotal river excavation project in Nellore, Andra Pradesh. This success opened doors for business opportunities like Coal excavation projects.
                 Everyone will face failure in life at different stages. No matter how careful we are. Failure knocks our door at times. One who learns from their mistakes and accepting failure as a stepping stone and part of life experience will achieve great success in life. MGM Anand is one such person , who believes in the above words, Although he is a great business man who has been running business profitably for years he too faced failure. He failed in the granite business he started. This failure made him understand that the welfare of his employees was depending on him. It helped him to take his other decisions carefully. He believed that when we commit a mistake it is better to speak it out and find a solution that to be silent on the matter.



Legacy

                 MGM Anand is a person who desires for innovation and creativity. His previous experiences in Chennai port helped him in the scrap metal business in Visakhapatnam port. An out of the box business idea bolstered the truck business. During his Visakhapatnam stay, he encountered a bank officer who reminds him the importance of time management and connecting with people. He worked hard with his employees and inspects the truck personally. He instituted rigorous reporting mechanism. This helps to monitor the profitability of each truck accurately, even in the days of no barcode and software based systems. He returned to Chennai after 7 years and took the declining resort business. With his patience and experience, he concentrates on providing the best service. He enhanced the Chinese food restaurant in the resort by offering beach extensions and named it as service on the beach. He prioritizes his employees and customer satisfaction; he knows that for a business to flourish, contentment of its employees is important. As expected, by the year 2000 the resort business started to flourish and the customer satisfaction was surpassing. MGM Anand firmly believes that dedication, discipline and optimistic approach in spite of failures bring success. MGM Anand is a living example of that, incessant hard work is the reason for his success. He firmly believes that a positive work culture is vital for the organization to sustain. With a renewed way of thinking he focused on the requisite of the client and superior customer service. As a result the MGM Group went through a drastic advancement ubiquitously, by establishing strong business partnerships globally.
